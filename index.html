Our Services

Complete Roofing and Exterior Solutions

From full roof replacements to skylights, siding, and gutters, our certified crew handles every aspect of your home's exterior protection.

Residential Roofing

Full roof replacements and new installations using premium Owens Corning and GAF shingles, backed by manufacturer warranties and our 47 years of expertise.

Flat Roofing

Commercial and residential flat roof systems including TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen. Versico and Elevate certified for long-lasting, leak-free performance.

Skylights

Authorized Velux dealer for skylight installation, replacement, and repair. Bring natural light into your home with energy-efficient, properly flashed skylights.

Siding

Vinyl, fiber cement, and wood siding installation and repair. We protect and transform your home's exterior with materials built to withstand Long Island weather.

Gutters & Leaders

Seamless aluminum gutter systems custom-fabricated on-site. Proper drainage protects your foundation, landscaping, and prevents costly water damage.

Roof Repair

Storm damage, leaks, missing shingles, and emergency repairs. We respond fast to protect your home and prevent further damage to your property.

About Nassau Roofers, Inc.

A Family Legacy Built on Every Roof

In 1978, John Reuter founded Nassau Roofers with a simple promise: treat every home like your own. Nearly five decades later, his children Megan Ihne (President) and Bryan carry that same standard across every project in Nassau and Suffolk Counties.

As an Owens Corning Preferred Contractor, GAF certified, and Velux authorized dealer, we bring manufacturer-backed warranties and top-tier materials to every job. Our BBB A+ rating since 2002 and 80+ five-star reviews reflect the consistency our Long Island neighbors have come to rely on.
